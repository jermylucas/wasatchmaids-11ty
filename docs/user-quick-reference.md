# Netlify CMS User Quick Reference

## Accessing the CMS

**URL**: `https://yoursite.com/admin/`

1. Click "Login"
2. Sign in with your Netlify Identity account
3. You'll see only your documents collection

## Uploading a Document

1. Click on your documents collection (e.g., "John's Documents")
2. Click "Create new" button
3. Fill in the fields:
   - **Title**: Document name
   - **Description**: Brief description
   - **Date**: Document date
   - **Document Type**: Select (document, scorecard, report, other)
   - **File**: Click to upload
   - **Body**: Optional notes
4. Click "Save" or "Publish"

## Viewing Documents

1. Click on your documents collection
2. Click on any document to view details
3. Download files by clicking the file link

## Editing Documents

1. Click on your documents collection
2. Click on the document you want to edit
3. Make changes
4. Click "Save" or "Publish"

## Deleting Documents

1. Click on your documents collection
2. Click on the document you want to delete
3. Click "Delete" button
4. Confirm deletion

## What You Can Do

✅ View your documents and scorecards
✅ Upload new documents
✅ Edit document details
✅ Delete your documents
✅ Download your files

## What You Cannot Do

❌ Access other users' documents
❌ Access blog posts
❌ Access how-to guides
❌ Modify site settings

## Need Help?

- Contact your admin for assistance
- See the full guide: [Multi-User Setup Guide](netlify-cms-multi-user-guide.md)

## Tips

- Use descriptive titles for easy searching
- Add descriptions to help identify documents
- Choose the correct document type for better organization
- Download files before editing to ensure you have the latest version
