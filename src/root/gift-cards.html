Give the Perfect Gift

Nothing beats the feeling of a clean home. Give this gift to someone in your life, and it's guaranteed to put a smile on their face.

Sending Your Gift

When you check out, your gift card will be sent via email either immediately, or on a scheduled date.

Using a Gift Card

Your gift card recipient will get a unique code they can use while booking a cleaning. It's easy, there is no card to lose, and the balance is tracked automatically. Gift cards can be applied to any of our services.

Want To Gift In Person?

If you want something to give in person, or something they can unwrap, email the gift card to yourself, and print it out. This will include the customized code and have everything they need to apply the gift card.
