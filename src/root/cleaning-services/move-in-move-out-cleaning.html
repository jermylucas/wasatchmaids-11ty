Why get a move-in/move-out clean?

A move-in/move-out cleaning is perfect for an empty home to make sure the residence is ready for the next family. Whether that next family is a renter, someone who just bought the home, or even you, getting a home cleaned while it's empty is perfect so you don't have to live in the previous owner's dust, and so you can get all those walls, cupboards, and appliances cleaned before putting your own stuff there.

When cleaning in an empty home, there are exposed walls, baseboards, and cupboards that are not usually available for standard cleanings. Once you unpack and finish moving, these areas may not be cleaned again for a long time. By trusting Wasatch Maids to do the move-in / move-out clean for you, then you can guarantee everything will be placed in a clean home.

Move-In and Move-Out Cleaning Services

  • Everything in our standard cleaning
  • Wet-wipe blinds, windowsills
  • Doors, door and window casings
  • Walls and wall spots (baseboards too)
  • Inside fridge and oven
  • Inside drawers and cupboards
  • Interior windows and window tracks

There are only a few, but still some things our moving cleans can't cover. This is due to insurance and equipment restrictions. If you have any of these requests, call or text us and we can get you in touch with a trusted professional.

What we can't clean during a moving clean

  • Professionally cleaning of carpets (we thoroughly vacuum, but can't remove stains)
  • Grout & tile cleaning (again, we thoroughly clean, but grout and tile may require additional work for stain removal)
  • Bio-hazards

Our cleans include everything you can expect for an empty home and these items are included by default. Still, if you would like to exclude any extras, you can easily do so while booking online, or over the phone.
