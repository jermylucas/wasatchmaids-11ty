Many people have different definitions of a move-in / move-out cleaning. Our moving cleans include everything in the standard and deep cleanings, plus many other considerations to make sure the residence is perfect for you, or the next family. Our professionally certified cleaners are extremely thorough and put in the extra work to make your home look new again. Still, you may find that you want a little bit more elbow grease in a specific area, or maybe it's been a while since your home has had a good clean. Our goal with our deep cleaning services is to spend extra time on the items that only need a cleaning every 6-12 months. For Wasatch Maids, a deep cleaning is everything in the standard cleaning, plus wet-wiping blinds, doors, light switches, AC vents, and power outlets.

These extra details are designed to get your home looking as good as possible. Deep cleaning is sometimes called spring cleaning or detail cleaning.

Deep cleaning is a great opportunity to look at adding extras like interior windows, cleaning inside the fridge and/or oven, and spot cleaning walls.
