Job Description

Here at Wasatch Maids, we hold our cleaners up to a high standard. Working as a team, being honest, having great communication, and being able to provide great customer service are all extremely important to us.

Work from home answering phones, emails, and solving customer and team member issues. Manage schedules, and help deliver on our #1 promise: extremely detailed and quality cleaning.

You'll be responsible not only for helping customer, but also the teams that clean for them. Setting expectations, solving issues, setting up schedules, and passing on feedback to the team.

Benefits

  • Weekly pay
  • Work with a company that cares
  • Set your working days
  • Management opportunity
  • Salary pay, plus a percentage of profit
  • Flexible work that is from home 99% of the time

Requirements

  • Respond to texts and calls quickly
  • Being self-reliant
  • Go above and beyond to deliver on our #1 promise
  • Ability to use logic and critical thinking skills
  • Great english written and verbal communication skills
  • Speak Spanish (fluency not required, just basic conversational)
  • Know the basics of operating a computer and smartphone

Responsibilities

  • Answer calls, texts, and emails in a timely manner
  • Be available betweeen 7:30am - 6:00pm
  • Set up appointments and make sales
  • Make a great impression with the customer
  • Detect if a customer may be an issue in the future and do what's needed to fix issues before they arrive
  • Set up and manage cleaner schedules based on their availability and the customer addresses

We're looking for someone who has logical and critical thinking skills as well as the skills to keep customers happy. If you can think for yourself and use problem solving skills this may be the job for you. Being able to deliver on our #1 promise means doing whatever is needed to ensure and detailed clean that meets all expectations.

This job is work from home 99% of the time (unless it requires you to individually help deliver on the #1 promise), so requires you to be able to manage your own time and get tasks done. There is a lot of flexibility, since some days there is a lot to do and other days not much at all. Being able to manage your time and stay motivated while working remotely is extremely important.

Bonus

  • You have sales or customer service experience
  • You are able to work sometimes on Saturdays
