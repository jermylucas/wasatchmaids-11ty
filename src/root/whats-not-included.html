Worker Safety

Heavy Objects

For liability and safety, our cleaners are unable to lift more than 30 pounds. If you require cleaning behind large furniture or appliances, please do your best to make accomodations beforehand

Things We Can't Clean

If an item is on this list, it's because of insurance coverage, worker safety, or that we simply don't have the equipment to do so. We will always do our best to help, so if you need something that's listed below, please reach out!

Note: The cleaning type determines the nature of things we can or cannot clean (for example, we don't clean paint on a standard clean, but we can do this during a construction clean or at an additional charge). If you have any questions, give us a call.

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

Your Satisfaction is guaranteed. While we do not offer refunds on services that have been completed, we will return & re-clean any areas of concern that are reported to us within 24 hours of your cleaning. We want this to be a help for you, and we will do everything we can to get back out there ASAP.
